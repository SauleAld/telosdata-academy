About Us

At Telos Data Academy, we believe a student's success is our success.

Information Technology market is crowded and to stand apart from competition, you need to continuously improve your skill set and deliver quality software.

Our management team has 20+ years experience in Software Quality Assurance and can add value to your career. We work with the latest technologies in the market and cover a wide range of skills. We also provide post training support to our students to enable them to succeed.
